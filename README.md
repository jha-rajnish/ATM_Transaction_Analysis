<h2 align="left">
📈Analysis Report🧐
</h2>

1. Amount withdrawn in 7-years:
   - 2011 - (₹767M/13%)📈
   - 2012 - (₹939M/16%)📈
   - 2013 - (₹1108M/18%)📈
   - 2014 - (₹1216M/20%)📈
   - 2015 - (₹896M/15%)📉
   - 2016 - (₹612M/10%)📉
   - 2017 - (₹515M/9%)📉
   <br></br>
  - Total Withdrawals (2011 - 2017) = **₹6 Billion**
<br></br>

2. Number of withdrawals in 7-years:
   - 2011 - (205K/14.31%)📈
   - 2012 - (232K/16.22%)📈
   - 2013 - (266K/18.58%)📈
   - 2014 - (280K/19.59%)📈
   - 2015 - (206K/14.44%)📉
   - 2016 - (129K/9.05%)📉
   - 2017 - (112K/7.81%)📉
   <br></br>
  - Total Number of Withdrawals (2011 - 2017) = **1429K**
<br></br>

3. Amount withdrawn by Master & Visa Card in 7-years:
   - 2011 - Master Card(₹240M/31.25%) & Visa Card(₹527M/68.75%)
   - 2012 - Master Card(₹297M/31.67%) & Visa Card(₹642M/68.34%)
   - 2013 - Master Card(₹377M/33.99%) & Visa Card(₹732M/66.01%)
   - 2014 - Master Card(₹462M/38.02%) & Visa Card(₹754M/61.98%)
   - 2015 - Master Card(₹325M/36.30%) & Visa Card(₹571M/63.70%)
   - 2016 - Master Card(₹227M/37.05%) & Visa Card(₹385M/62.95%)
   - 2017 - Master Card(₹248M/48.12%) & Visa Card(₹267M/51.88%)
   <br></br>
  - Total Withdrawals by Master Card (2011 - 2017) = **₹2.18 Billion (35.95%)**
  - Total Withdrawals by Visa Card (2011 - 2017) = **₹3.88 Billion (64.05%)**
<br></br>

4. Number of withdrawals at various ATMs by Master & Visa Card:
   - Airport ATM - (205K/14.32%)
   - Big Street ATM - (207K/14.49%)
   - Christ College ATM - (291K/20.37%)
   - KK Nagar ATM - (402K/28.11%)
   - Mount Road ATM - (325K/22.71%)
<br></br>
   - Airport ATM - Master Card(52K/25.64%) & Visa Card(152K/74.36%)
   - Big Street ATM - Master Card(138K/66.78%) & Visa Card(69K/33.22%)
   - Christ College ATM - Master Card(172K/59.15%) & Visa Card(119K/40.85%)
   - KK Nagar ATM - Master Card(113K/28.18%) & Visa Card(289K/71.82%)
   - Mount Road ATM - Master Card(170K/52.41%) & Visa Card(154K/47.59%)
<br></br>
  - Total Number of Withdrawals by Master Card (2011 - 2017) = **646K/45.22%**
  - Total Withdrawals by Visa Card (2011 - 2017) = **783K/54.78%**
<br></br>
